Frequently asked questions (FAQ)

For how long can I borrow a book?

Books can be borrowed for 21 days. This period can be extended by one week at a time, with a maximum of three weeks. Extending is free of charge and can only be done if no one else made a reservation for that particular book. The status of your books can be checked on your personal library account

How can I reserve a book?

Search for the book via your Library Catalog and click the "Reserve" button. A reservations is valid for 7 days and it is free. If the book is not picked-up after 7 days, the reservation will automatically be canceled.

What happens if I can't return a book on time?

A fine of EUR 0,10 per book is charged per day. When keeping a book in your possession that should have been returned, reservations cannot be made and new orders cannot be picked up.
